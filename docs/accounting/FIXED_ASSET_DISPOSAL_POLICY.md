# Fixed-asset disposal and write-off policy

Disposal is a posted sale workflow with proceeds and an explicit proceeds account. Write-off is the same lifecycle without proceeds. Both clear the asset cost and accumulated depreciation balances, recognize the calculated gain or loss through the category mappings, stop future unposted schedule lines, create a movement, and record source journal evidence.

Reversal requires the disposed or written-off asset to have no later movement. It posts a balanced reversal, restores the asset status and carrying values, and records the reversal movement. Destructive deletion is not used.
