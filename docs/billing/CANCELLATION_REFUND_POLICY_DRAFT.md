# Cancellation and Refund Policy Draft

Status: draft, requires legal/commercial review
Date: 2026-07-02

## Draft Policy Questions

- Are subscriptions month-to-month or annual?
- Are refunds prorated?
- What happens to data after cancellation?
- How long is read-only access retained?
- What export format is guaranteed?
- How are compliance/eInvoice artifacts retained after cancellation?
- Who approves goodwill refunds?

## Conservative Draft Position

For controlled beta, no paid billing is active. For future paid beta, cancellation and refund terms must be accepted in Terms of Service and reflected in payment provider configuration before payment collection starts.
