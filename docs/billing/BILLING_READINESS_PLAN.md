# Billing Readiness Plan

Status: plan only, no payment collection
Date: 2026-07-02

## Goal

Prepare paid-beta billing decisions without enabling billing, integrating a payment provider, or collecting money in this PR.

## Required Before Paid Beta

- pricing model approved
- plan entitlements documented
- trial/beta conversion policy
- invoices/receipts for LedgerByte subscription fees
- cancellation/refund policy approved
- tax/VAT handling for LedgerByte fees reviewed
- payment provider selected and security reviewed
- legal terms/privacy approved
- support and SLA boundaries documented

## Non-Goals

- No Stripe/payment provider integration.
- No subscription database schema change.
- No payment collection.
- No paid launch readiness claim.
