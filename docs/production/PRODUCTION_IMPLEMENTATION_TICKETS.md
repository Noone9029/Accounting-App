## Ticket Field Guide

- Stage: `controlled beta`, `paid private beta`, `public production`, or `ZATCA gate`.
- Owner discipline: backend, frontend, infra, security, accounting, legal, product, support, QA, or ops.
- Priority: `P0` blocks paid/public launch, `P1` reduces major launch risk, `P2` is required for advertised capability or scale, `P3` is polish/follow-up.
- Commands/tests expected: future ticket validation only. Do not run live commands unless the future ticket explicitly approves the target environment and data scope.
