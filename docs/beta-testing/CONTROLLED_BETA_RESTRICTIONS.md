# Controlled Beta Restrictions

Date: 2026-07-01

Status: Required tester-facing restriction draft. This is product/legal draft wording, not final legal advice.

## Core Restriction

LedgerByte is available only as a controlled beta for approved testers. It is not public launch, production launch, compliance launch, or paid SaaS launch.

## Data Restriction

Use test/demo data only unless the product owner separately approves a narrow data policy in writing.

Testers must not enter sensitive production financial data, production customer data, production supplier data, raw bank files, payroll, contracts, IDs, tax filings, signed XML, QR payloads, provider credentials, or real attachment/document bodies.

## No Legal, Tax, Filing, or Audit Reliance

Beta outputs, reports, statements, PDFs, ledgers, document archives, readiness panels, and dashboards are for product feedback only. They must not be used for official accounting close, audit evidence, tax filing, legal compliance, customer statements, supplier statements, or authority submission.

## Compliance Restrictions

LedgerByte does not currently provide:

- ZATCA production compliance.
- UAE production compliance.
- Peppol certification.
- ASP connectivity.
- Real provider submission.
- Real ASP validation or submission.
- CSID request, OTP capture, clearance, reporting, or tax authority submission.
- PDF/A-3 or signed XML production reliance.

Compliance pages are readiness/review surfaces only.

## Banking Restrictions

LedgerByte does not currently provide:

- Live bank feeds.
- Bank aggregation.
- Real bank API connectivity.
- Automatic reconciliation as a production claim.

Banking and reconciliation review is manual/test-data only in this beta.

## Payment and Email Restrictions

LedgerByte does not currently provide:

- Real payment collection.
- Paid billing or production subscriptions.
- Production customer email sending guarantees.

Do not send real customer emails or collect real payments during the beta.

## Storage and Document Restrictions

LedgerByte does not currently provide:

- Production object-storage proof.
- Production signed-URL proof.
- Production backup/restore proof for tester data.
- Production retention/legal-hold guarantee.

Documents and archives should be treated as beta review surfaces only.

## Tester Duties

Testers must:

- Report bugs and confusing behavior.
- Stop and report any blocker, security/privacy concern, misleading production claim, or cross-organization data exposure.
- Avoid destructive actions unless the walkthrough explicitly allows them in a disposable test organization.
- Redact screenshots/videos.
- Accept that access may be revoked at any time.

## Expansion Restriction

Do not expand beyond the initial 3 to 5 approved testers until:

- Blocker and high issues are zero.
- Access revocation has been tested manually for the cohort.
- Feedback has been triaged.
- Support capacity is confirmed.
- Owner approval is recorded.
