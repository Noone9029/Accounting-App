## Cross-Branch Local Evidence Summary

- DEV-08 through DEV-08M provide strong local-only evidence for AP state-machine behavior, AP-adjacent purchase/inventory paths, generated-document output/archive/download behavior, AP output permissions, AP generated-document mock outbox behavior, fiscal-period blockers, permission denials, and cleanup retention posture.
- The evidence is local and fixture/marker scoped. It is not production evidence, beta evidence, hosted/shared-target evidence, customer-data evidence, or accountant/compliance certification.
- Mutation evidence was constrained to explicitly approved local parts in the underlying branches. This DEV-08Z Part 1 consolidation performed documentation-only review.
- Output evidence is metadata/hash/count oriented. It does not expose or validate PDF bodies, attachment bodies, email bodies, provider payloads, signed XML, QR payloads, secrets, auth headers, tokens, cookies, database URLs, or customer/vendor data.
- Cleanup posture is preserve-by-default. Generated-document rows, outbox rows, provider rows, audit logs, journals, allocations, source AP documents, receipts, stock movements, users/roles/memberships, and ZATCA metadata/logs remain evidence unless a future approved cleanup branch defines otherwise.

## Remaining Production And Beta Gaps

- Linked PO-to-bill receipt reconciliation and purchase matching remain production gaps.
- Valuation variance booking, landed cost, returns, FIFO/cost-layer policy, serial/batch/bin/location, and broader inventory accounting policy remain future work.
- Real provider email delivery, retry scheduling, suppression/bounce handling, provider webhooks, domain/DKIM/SPF/DMARC policy, monitoring, and production support remain unproven.
- Duplicate generated-document rows are currently treated as append-only versioned archive behavior for paid v1; future UX can add latest filtering, explicit version labels, or supersession only after legal/audit retention and document-list behavior are designed together.
- Cleanup execution remains unimplemented by policy. DEV-08M created only a local dry-run-only count planner.
- Broad AP browser E2E, full smoke, full build, full test, load/concurrency, cross-tenant, restricted-role matrix, and deployed/beta verification remain outside this evidence chain.
- Production, beta/user-testing, hosted/shared database, customer-data, real ZATCA network/signing/clearance/reporting/PDF-A3, and accountant/compliance certification remain unproven.
