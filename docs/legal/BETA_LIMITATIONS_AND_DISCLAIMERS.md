# Beta Limitations and Disclaimers

Status: controlled beta language draft
Date: 2026-07-02

## Controlled Beta Position

LedgerByte is suitable only for controlled beta under restrictions. It is not public production-ready.

## Required Disclaimers

- No production hosting decision is final.
- No UAE compliance claim is made.
- No Peppol certification claim is made.
- No accredited ASP connectivity is active.
- No real ASP, Peppol, or FTA submission is available.
- ZATCA/UAE workflows are local readiness and controlled evidence only unless separately approved.
- Live bank feeds and automatic reconciliation are not claimed.
- Object storage, signed URLs, and hosted PITR require proof before production claims.
- Billing/legal documents are drafts and require review before payment collection.

## Support Framing

Beta users should report workflow blockers, UX defects, and readiness gaps. Support must not promise compliance outcomes or production SLAs.
