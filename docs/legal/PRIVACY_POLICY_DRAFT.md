# Privacy Policy Draft

Status: draft, requires legal review
Date: 2026-07-02

This draft identifies privacy topics for review. It is not approved for production or paid launch.

## Data Categories

- account identity: name, email, organization membership
- organization profile: business name, address, tax identifiers
- accounting data: contacts, invoices, bills, payments, journals, reports
- operational data: audit logs, support requests, health/readiness evidence
- generated artifacts: PDFs, XML/readiness outputs, attachments
- future provider data: ASP submission payloads/responses only after approved integration

## Draft Commitments

- Use data to provide accounting workspace features and support.
- Keep secrets and credentials out of support logs and docs.
- Restrict access by organization and role.
- Use provider integrations only after explicit configuration and review.
- Retain regulated artifacts according to reviewed policy.

## Review Gaps

- jurisdiction-specific privacy law
- subprocessors
- data residency
- data subject request workflow
- breach notification timing
- retention/deletion periods
